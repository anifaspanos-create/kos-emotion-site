The Experience

Glide along the coast as the island exhales and the light turns to gold. This isn't just a bike ride — it's a slow, quiet journey through the corners that make Kos unforgettable, led by someone who calls this island home. Effortless pedalling, premium e-bikes, and one simple promise: the most beautiful sunset on the island, from the most beautiful spot.

  • Duration: about 2 hours
  • Departs from Lampi Beach
  • Start time follows the sunset (varies by season)

  • From €35/ person

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing and closed or flat shoes are best for cycling. Evenings by the sea can be breezy, so bring a light layer.

What happens if it rains?

Your safety comes first. If the weather doesn't cooperate, we'll reschedule your tour to another available time or offer a full refund — whichever you prefer.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your tour for a full refund.

Is the tour insured?

Yes, every tour is covered by liability insurance for your peace of mind.

Do I need cycling experience?

Not at all — our e-bikes make pedaling effortless, and the pace is relaxed and suitable for all fitness levels.

What if I need a specific bike size?

We have bikes in multiple sizes to fit every height — just let us know when booking.

What if the bike breaks down or I get a flat tire?

Just call us — we'll get you back on the road or swap your bike as quickly as possible.

Is a helmet included?

Yes, every rental includes a helmet.

Is there a minimum age to rent?

There's no strict age limit — just make sure a responsible adult is with younger riders.
